Hello, welcome to the web hosted version of my app Train Me. I built the beta version back in high school, but have been using and improving it ever since. In 2021, the beta won third place in the Maine App Challenge hosted by Tyler Technologies. The following year I completely overhauled the app and won second out of over 100+ submissions!

I prefer to use the app on my phone and have logged over 160 workouts since implementing the accounts tracking system. The app was built using JavaScript, HTML, and all graphic design, code, and UX were done by me. Code.org still handles the backend, which is why you may have had to input your age.
